The meaning of life is a very hard thing to get an actual answer too. While I might have my own thoughts of what means to live I am 100% certain it isn't the only answer since after all, those are just my ideals.

So instead, why don't I talk about the meaning of love?

What does the word love mean?

Scientifically love is a sort of chemical, to those people love is nothing but a value among other objects in the world, just another molecule around more atoms. For such reasons they can't enjoy it as it's meant to be.

Love is more than just a feeling or an emotion, to love is to see a person and admire the perfect imperfections in them, be it a friend, family member or even a loved one. Love can be compared to a tool, is a gateway to living life a different way. Through love you make relationships that change and mold your life, hopefully for the better.

You share smiles, laughs, cries and memories all through this medium of emotions. Love is but a way of living and providing your life more meaning than it already has. So that one day, when you're old enough and laying on the bed you can finally say, I swear, I lived.

End notes

Hopefully that can provide a brief summary. Though, Love is like the universe, ever expanding and filled with information.

It matters not what you've done, but what you do with what you've done for others.
